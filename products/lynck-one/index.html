Lynck Intelligence — 01

AI Setup Engine

Log your setups and how each one felt. The Setup Engine runs on a custom model trained on thousands of real karting sessions — combined with your own history and the live track conditions, it hands you the ideal setup before you roll out: axle, seat, tyre pressures, gearing, ride height.

You bring the feedback. It brings the setup sheet.

Lynck Intelligence — 02

AI Data Coach

Trained on thousands of laps from thousands of drivers, it reads every lap against your data and an ideal reference, then tells you in plain words: "you're faster when you brake later into turn 4 — on average you brake ~6m too early and lose 0.18s there." Concrete, corner-by-corner.

After each session it builds a report — "try this next time", or "you nailed it lap 7, then drifted off" — so every run makes you quicker.

13 — FAQ

Karting data, answered.

Everything you might ask before choosing a karting data logger, lap timer or AI coach.

What is the best karting data logger?
Lynck One is a complete karting data logger that records 14 channels at up to 2000Hz with centimetre-accurate positioning, a sunlight-readable transflective display, a GPS lap timer and built-in AI setup and coaching. Where most loggers charge separately for every sensor, Lynck includes the full system in one price from €519.
Is Lynck a good alternative to MyChron, AiM, Alfano or Starlane?
Yes. Compared to a MyChron 6, AiM MXS, Alfano 6 or Starlane Davinci, Lynck One offers more data channels, more accurate positioning, a normally-black transflective screen that stays readable in direct sunlight, and AI coaching — usually at a lower total price than a comparably equipped competitor setup. One system, everything included.
Does Lynck One work as a GPS lap timer?
Absolutely. Lynck One has a built-in GPS for precise lap and sector times, combined with the Lynck KDS precision engine that keeps tracking your position continuously around the lap — so your racing line and lap data stay accurate to the centimetre, not just at each GPS point.
What data channels does it record?
Lynck One logs 14 channels in total — covering speed and engine data, driver inputs, grip and track conditions, plus lap and sector timing and GPS position. Everything is logged together and time-synced for full analysis.
What does the AI setup engine and data coach do?
The AI Setup Engine runs on a custom model trained on thousands of real karting sessions: log how the kart felt and it hands you an ideal setup — axle, seat, tyre pressures, gearing, ride height — based on your history and the live track conditions. The AI Data Coach reads every lap against an ideal reference and tells you in plain words where you lose time and how much, then builds a report after each session.
How much does Lynck One cost?
Lynck One starts at €519 for the complete base system. You can add storage upgrades and front/rear sensor modules in the configurator. There are no hidden per-sensor fees — what others charge for a single sensor, Lynck charges for the complete system.
Does it work offline and is my data private?
Yes. Lynck One logs and works fully offline — the data logger never needs an internet connection. You can keep your data completely private and still log, review and analyse every session yourself. Only the AI features (Setup Engine and Data Coach) are tied to a subscription tier.
