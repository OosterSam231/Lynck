01 — The Team

Small team.
Big ambition.

Lynck Racing — officially the Oosterlynck Racing Team — is a two-person operation: one driver, one crew, and a shared obsession with finding lap time the hard way.

We run lean against bigger, better-funded rivals. That constraint is exactly why Lynck exists: every sensor, every line of firmware and every AI model is built and tested here first, on our own kart, before it ever reaches another driver.
